About The Haven

With 15 shelters across Cape Town and surrounding areas, we focus on physical care, social services, personal development, and family reunification, aiming to help clients transition from street life to a stable home.

Our Shelters

15 Shelters Across Cape Town

Our network of shelters provides safe, dignified accommodation and support services to help individuals transition off the streets.

  • Emergency accommodation
  • Meals and basic necessities
  • Social support services
  • Skills development programs
