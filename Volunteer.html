Why Volunteer?

Volunteering with The Haven means becoming part of a compassionate community dedicated to restoring dignity and hope. Your time and skills make a direct, meaningful impact in helping individuals transition from homelessness to stable housing.

Frequently Asked Questions

We appreciate any time you can offer! Most volunteers commit to 2-4 hours per week, but we can work with your schedule.

Most volunteer roles require no special qualifications, just enthusiasm and compassion. We provide training for all positions.

Volunteers must be at least 18 years old. We do have special programs for younger volunteers with parental consent.
